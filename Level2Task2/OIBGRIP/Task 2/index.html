Sidhu Moose Wala

1993-2022

Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu, better known by his stage name Sidhu Moose Wala,was an Indian singer, rapper, songwriter and actor associated with Punjabi music and Punjabi cinema. Sidhu rose to the mainstream with his track "So High". In 2018, he released his debut album PBX 1, which peaked at number 66 on the Billboard Canadian Albums chart. Early Life

Born in Moosa, Mansa district, Punjab, Moose Wala started in 2016 as a songwriter for the song "License" by Ninja, and as singer in 2017 with a duet song, "G Wagon". Following his debut, he collaborated with Brown Boyz for various tracks. His numourous tracks have peaked on the UK Asian chart, four of which have topped the chart. His song "Bambiha Bole" was among the top five on the Global YouTube music chart. In 2021, he released Moosetape, tracks from which charted globally including Billboard Global 200, Billboard Global Excl. US, Canadian Hot 100, UK Asian, and New Zealand Hot charts. He has the most number-one singles on the Billboard India Songs chart.
